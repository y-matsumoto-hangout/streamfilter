How to Use

  1. Go to streamfilter.tv
  2. Paste any video URL and click Play
  3. Click "Infrared" or "Night Vision" in the Presets section
  4. Fine-tune the Hue Rotate slider for different color variations

Pro Tips

  • Hue Rotate at 90° — Creates a purple/blue cold infrared look
  • Hue Rotate at 270° — Creates a warm amber/orange thermal look
  • Night Vision + 0.5x speed — Slow-motion tactical replay effect
  • Infrared + high saturation (400%+) — Extreme psychedelic visuals

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply an infrared filter to a video?

Open streamfilter.tv, paste any video URL, and click the "Infrared" preset. This applies a 180° hue rotation with high saturation and contrast, creating a thermal camera effect instantly.

What's the difference between Infrared and Night Vision filters?

The Infrared filter shifts colors by 180° with high saturation, mimicking a thermal imaging camera. The Night Vision filter uses a 120° hue shift to create a green-tinted military scope effect with high contrast.

Can I use these filters on live streams?

Yes! Both Infrared and Night Vision filters work in real time on YouTube live streams, Twitch streams, and any other supported video. The filters are applied instantly as the video plays.
