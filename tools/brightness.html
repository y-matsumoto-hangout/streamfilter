Why Adjust Video Brightness?

Many videos are recorded in poor lighting conditions, making them difficult to watch. Instead of straining your eyes, you can increase the brightness in real time while the video plays. Stream Filter lets you do this without downloading software or uploading files.

Common Use Cases

Dark Videos
Increase brightness to see details in poorly lit footage.
Night Viewing
Lower brightness to reduce eye strain when watching at night.
Outdoor Viewing
Boost brightness to see your screen in sunlight.
Accessibility
Help those with visual impairments see content more clearly.

How It Works

  1. Go to streamfilter.tv
  2. Paste any video URL (YouTube, Twitch, Vimeo, Dailymotion, and more)
  3. Use the Brightness slider to adjust from 30% to 500%
  4. Or click the "Bright" preset for an instant boost

All processing happens in your browser — your video data never leaves your device.

Brightness Range

  • 30% — Very dark, for extreme night mode
  • 100% — Default, original brightness
  • 200% — Noticeably brighter, good for dark videos
  • 500% — Maximum boost for the darkest footage

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a dark YouTube video brighter?

Paste the YouTube URL into Stream Filter and increase the Brightness slider. You can also try the "Bright" preset for an instant boost. No upload or software needed — it works directly in your browser.

Does adjusting brightness affect video quality?

No. Stream Filter applies CSS filters on top of the video player, so the original video quality is not affected. You're just changing how it's displayed on your screen.

Can I adjust brightness on mobile?

Yes, Stream Filter is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets. Just open streamfilter.tv in your mobile browser.

What range of brightness adjustment is available?

You can adjust brightness from 30% (very dark) to 500% (extremely bright), giving you full control over the video's visibility.
