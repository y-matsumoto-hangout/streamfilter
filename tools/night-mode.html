Frequently Asked Questions

How do I watch YouTube in night mode?

Open streamfilter.tv, paste any YouTube URL, and click the "Night Mode" preset. This automatically reduces brightness, lowers saturation, and adds a slight warm tone — perfect for watching in the dark without straining your eyes.

Does YouTube have a built-in night mode for videos?

YouTube's Dark Theme only changes the website background to dark — it does not affect the video itself. Stream Filter's Night Mode actually dims and warms the video content, which is more effective at reducing eye strain.

Is watching videos at night bad for your eyes?

Bright screens in dark environments can cause eye strain, headaches, and disrupt sleep. Reducing brightness and adding warm tones (like Stream Filter's Night Mode) helps minimize these effects by reducing blue light and overall screen intensity.

What's the difference between Night Mode and Night Vision?

Night Mode reduces brightness and adds warm tones for comfortable viewing. Night Vision applies a green-tinted military scope effect with high contrast — it's a fun visual filter, not designed for eye comfort.

How do I set up YouTube Dark Mode on PC?

YouTube's Dark Theme only changes the page background — it doesn't dim the actual video. To dim the video itself, use Stream Filter: paste any YouTube URL and click the 'Night Mode' preset. This reduces brightness to 70%, lowers saturation, and adds warm tones for comfortable viewing.

Can't turn off YouTube Dark Mode — what should I do?

If YouTube is stuck in dark mode, go to YouTube Settings > Appearance > Light theme. But note that this only affects the page background, not the video brightness. If your issue is that videos look too dark, try Stream Filter to boost brightness independently.

What's the best setting for eye-friendly YouTube viewing?

For the most comfortable viewing, use Stream Filter's Night Mode preset (brightness 70%, saturation 80%, sepia 10%). This reduces blue light and overall intensity. For even more comfort, combine it with lowering your device's screen brightness.

Does YouTube Dark Mode save battery?

YouTube's Dark Theme can save battery on OLED screens by using more black pixels. Stream Filter's Night Mode goes further by also dimming the video content itself, reducing total light output from your screen even more.
