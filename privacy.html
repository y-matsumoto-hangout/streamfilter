Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Overview

Stream Filter ("we", "our", "the Service") is a free online tool that allows users to adjust video display settings. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Stream Filter is a client-side application. All video filter adjustments are processed entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • YouTube URLs or video viewing history
  • Filter settings or preferences
  • IP addresses or device identifiers

Third-Party Services

Our Service embeds YouTube videos using YouTube's official embed player. When you load a video, your browser communicates directly with YouTube's servers. YouTube's own privacy policy applies to that interaction. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

We may use third-party advertising services in the future. If implemented, these services may use cookies or similar technologies. This policy will be updated accordingly.

Cookies

Stream Filter itself does not set any cookies. However, the embedded YouTube player and any third-party advertising services may set their own cookies.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
